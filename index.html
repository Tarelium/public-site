What is Tarelium?

Purpose-built accelerators for higher education

Tarelium is a suite of intelligent, Microsoft-native accelerators that transform how universities and colleges manage the complete student lifecycle - from recruitment through graduation and beyond.

Delivered by Bayshann with deep sector expertise in higher education, health care, and aged care - backed by over a decade of trusted institutional partnerships across Australia.
